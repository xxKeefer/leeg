## Description

Compute per-Pokemon and per-trainer statistics from parsed battle data. Aggregate across games, matches, and the season.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Per-Pokemon stats: damage dealt, damage taken, KOs, faints, turns survived, move usage breakdown (attack/status/setup/support)
- [ ] Per-trainer stats: wins, losses, series W/L record
- [ ] Stats aggregate across all games in a season
- [ ] Stat leaderboards rank Pokemon and trainers by key metrics
